Aaron Lopez

Aaron Lopez is based in Atlanta, Georgia, and works as a Product Specialist for a turnkey EV charging startup. He is also an aspiring software developer with ambitions to become a future Product Manager.

Aaron has a lot he hopes to experience and achieve. He dreams of spending time on the road, where he can immerse himself in coding while traveling. Though not a permanent lifestyle, life on the road is a key item on his bucket list.

Aaron Enjoys Running. Running is an excellent way to clear your head or catch up on your favorite TV shows. Whether on a treadmill with a screen for entertainment or outdoors in a park to enjoy nature, running offers a range of enjoyment. Each environment has its own appeal, indoor running allows for catching up on your favorite shows, while outdoor running lets you immerse yourself in nature.

Currently, Aaron devotes most of his time to his career. Outside of work and coding studies, he stays active through weightlifting and running. Running on the treadmill doubles as a chance to catch up on his favorite shows, like The Lincoln Lawyer. Occasionally, Aaron spends time with friends, usually nearby, playing card games or joining a poker night. He also enjoys early 2000s sitcoms as his go-to comfort shows.
